# Roadmap

> **Vision 2030:** GENIUS GROUPS SAS will be the most trusted digital infrastructure provider in Africa — the layer that every African startup, enterprise, and government builds on.

---

## Strategic Axes

Our roadmap is organized around **six strategic axes** that span all products. Each axis has a multi-year vision with specific milestones.

| Axis | Vision | Lead Product(s) |
|---|---|---|
| 💳 **Payments** | Universal payment infrastructure for Africa | GeniusPay™ |
| 🤖 **AI** | AI-native experiences for African businesses | Genius AI™ |
| 🔐 **Identity** | Trusted digital identity for 100M+ Africans | Genius Identity™ |
| ✍️ **Digital Trust** | Verifiable, legally binding digital interactions | Genius Docs Sign™, Genius Invoice™ |
| ☁️ **Cloud** | African sovereign cloud platform | Genius Cloud™, Genius Runtime™ |
| 🛠️ **Developer Platform** | The best developer experience in Africa | All products + SDKs |

## How We Prioritize

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **User impact** | 40% | How many users benefit, and how much |
| **Revenue potential** | 20% | Direct or indirect revenue generation |
| **Strategic alignment** | 20% | Alignment with Vision 2030 |
| **Technical feasibility** | 10% | Can we build it with current team and tech |
| **Regulatory requirement** | 10% | Mandatory compliance or legal obligation |

## Contributing to the Roadmap

The roadmap is a living document. It is reviewed quarterly by the Core Team and updated based on:

- User feedback (GitHub Discussions, support tickets, user research)
- Market analysis (competitive landscape, regulatory changes)
- Technical discoveries (new capabilities, constraints)
- Business priorities (funding, partnerships, strategic shifts)

### Proposing a Change

1. Open a [Discussion](https://github.com/orgs/GENIUS-GROUPS-SAS/discussions) in the **Ideas & Feature Requests** category.
2. Describe the proposal, the problem it solves, and the expected impact.
3. If the proposal gains community support, it will be reviewed by the Core Team at the next quarterly planning session.
4. Accepted proposals are added to the roadmap with a target year and status.
