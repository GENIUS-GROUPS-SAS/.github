# Community

Welcome to the GENIUS GROUPS SAS community. This document is your guide to who we are, how we communicate, and how you can be part of building African-first digital infrastructure.

## Our Community Vision

> **A community where African developers, entrepreneurs, and global contributors collaborate to build technology that solves real African problems.**

We believe that the best products are built in the open, with input from the people who use them. Whether you're a developer in Abidjan, a designer in Dakar, or a student in Accra — you belong here.

## How to Get Involved

### For Developers

| Level | How to Start | What You Can Do |
|---|---|---|
| **Newcomer** | Read [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md) | Fix typos, improve docs, tackle `good first issue` |
| **Regular Contributor** | Consistent PRs over 1+ months | Implement features, review PRs, triage issues |
| **Maintainer** | Nominated by Lead Maintainer | Merge PRs, cut releases, mentor contributors |

### For Designers

- Contribute to our design system
- Propose UI/UX improvements via Discussions
- Create wireframes and mockups for feature requests
- Review accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)

### For Technical Writers

- Improve documentation (open a documentation issue)
- Write tutorials and guides
- Translate documentation (FR ↔ EN)
- Review API documentation for clarity

### For Product Managers

- Propose features via Discussions
- Conduct user research and share findings
- Help prioritize issues and feature requests
- Contribute to roadmap discussions

### For Students & Learners

- Join our [Certification Program](#certification-program)
- Tackle `good first issue` and `help wanted` issues
- Ask questions in Q&A Discussions
- Build projects using Genius APIs and share in Show & Tell

## Certification Program

GENIUS GROUPS SAS operates a **Product Engineer Certification Program** for students and professionals.

| Certification | Description | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| **Product Engineer** | Full-stack product development — discovery to deployment | 15 weeks |
| **Product Engineer — FinTech** | Specialization in payment integration and OHADA compliance | 15 + 4 weeks |
| **Product Engineer — AI** | Specialization in AI-assisted document generation | 15 + 4 weeks |

### How to Apply

1. Visit [geniusgroups.org/certification](https://geniusgroups.org/certification) *(coming soon)*
2. Submit an application with your background and motivation
3. Selected candidates are assigned to a cohort
4. Cohorts start quarterly (January, April, July, October)

### What You Build

Certification candidates build a **real product** integrated with Genius infrastructure:

- GeniusPay payment integration
- Electronic signature module
- Quote-to-payment workflow
- API, SDK, documentation, deployment, go-to-market

> The certification is awarded by a jury of GENIUS GROUPS leadership, external experts, and pedagogical advisors.

## Code of Conduct

Our community is governed by the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Everyone — maintainers, contributors, and users — is expected to uphold it.

### Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates the Code of Conduct:

1. **Report it** to [conduct@geniusgroups.org](mailto:conduct@geniusgroups.org)
2. **Provide details** — what happened, when, who was involved, any evidence
3. **Trust the process** — all reports are reviewed confidentially and acted upon

We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or toxicity in any form.

## Community Guidelines

### Do

- **Be welcoming** — greet newcomers, answer questions patiently
- **Be respectful** — of differing opinions, experience levels, and backgrounds
- **Be constructive** — give actionable feedback, not just criticism
- **Be collaborative** — share knowledge, help others, build together
- **Be patient** — everyone starts somewhere
- **Be curious** — ask questions, explore, learn

### Don't

- **Don't be toxic** — no insults, harassment, or personal attacks
- **Don't spam** — no self-promotion without context, no off-topic posts
- **Don't gatekeep** — don't tell people they don't belong
- **Don't plagiarize** — credit others' work
- **Don't derail** — keep discussions on topic

## FAQ

### How do I start contributing?

Read [CONTRIBUTING.md](./CONTRIBUTING.md), find a `good first issue`, and open a PR. We're here to help.

### Do I need to be in Africa to contribute?

No. We welcome contributors from anywhere in the world. Our focus is Africa, but our community is global.

### I'm a student. Can I contribute?

Absolutely. Many of our contributors are students. Start with documentation improvements or small bug fixes, and work your way up.

### Can my company partner with GENIUS GROUPS?

Yes. Email [hello@geniusgroups.org](mailto:hello@geniusgroups.org) with your partnership proposal.

### How do I report a bug?

Open an issue using the [Bug Report template](https://github.com/GENIUS-GROUPS-SAS/.github/issues/new?template=bug_report.yml).

### How do I request a feature?

Start a [Discussion](https://github.com/orgs/GENIUS-GROUPS-SAS/discussions) in the **Ideas & Feature Requests** category. If it gains support, we'll ask you to open a formal feature request issue.
