## Becoming a Maintainer

If you're interested in becoming a maintainer:

1. **Contribute consistently** — high-quality PRs, thorough reviews, helpful discussions.
2. **Demonstrate expertise** — show deep understanding of the product and architecture.
3. **Be active** — participate in discussions, triage issues, help newcomers.
4. **Express interest** — reach out to the current Lead Maintainer via email or discussions.

See [GOVERNANCE.md §3](./GOVERNANCE.md#3-maintainers) for the full criteria and process.

---

> **GENIUS GROUPS SAS** — Maintainers are stewards, not gatekeepers. Their job is to enable great contributions, not block them.
